Places to shop in Dublin

Let's go shopping!

Shopping in Dublin is a very enjoyable experience, nearly the whole city's shopping is pedestrianized and easy to stroll around.

Whether you're hunting for the perfect souvenir, looking for gifts for family and friends, or to add some of the latest fashion and accessories to your wardrobe, Dublin's got it covered.

Discover Dublin's beautiful lifestyle store with us.

1. Brown Thomas

The crème de la crème of department stores, Brown Thomas offers the most luxurious in clothing, cosmetics and homeware. This is where Bobbi Brown and Chanel nestle side by side amongst Tiffany’s and Louis Vuitton with a delicious café on the top floor that rejuvenates whilst simultaneously delighting taste buds.

2. Dublin Vintage Factory

Dublin Vintage Factory has been in the business for about 5 years and is known for top-quality vintage clothes. You can get clothing and accessories for 20 euro anytime – it really doesn’t get better than that! Oh wait! They’re also open 7 days a week. They have also recently started selling vintage homewares so why not spice up your home!

3. Angels Boutique

Are you looking for something unique and don’t mind spending a bit more money? Well Angels Boutique is the shop for you. Their goal is to ‘create and bring you amazing fashion fit for only angels.’ You can find everything here – dresses, two-pieces, jumpsuits, fur coats, name it! What are you waiting for?

4. Siopaella

The name ‘Siopella’ is coined from the Irish translation of ‘Ella’s shop.’ Siopella is a designer exchange store for new and used items ranging from clothes to handbags etc You can be guaranteed of the authenticity of their products.
