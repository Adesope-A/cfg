Entertainments

Let's Partyyyy!

What is some cool things you can do in Dublin? When we say entertainment, most of the time we are referring to PARTY!!!

Here's some cool bars and pubs to hang out with friends at night.

You will experience the amazing nightlife in Dublin.

1. Dicey's Garden

Dicey’s Garden is a popular venue of town among students. They are well-known for their cheap drinks. Their carvery is open weekdays from 12pm to 2.30pm and they offer €5 lunch for students. They also have a salad bar and soups available. They also provide late BBQ menu from 10pm where all items are just €2. It is a great place to hang out at night and to have insight about nightlife in Dublin.

2. Devitt's Pub

Devitt’s Pub which is located at Camden Street, is one of the top venues for a real traditional Irish music session. It is popular with the musicians and the public alike and usually attracts a number of varied and seriously good musicians that like to play together. Therefore, the bar provides a great night’s entertainment that is very lively and often frenetic.
